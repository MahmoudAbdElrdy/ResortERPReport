TabStrip / Tab Placement

{{tab.body}}







This sample demonstrates positioning of tabs on different sides. The TabStrip directive has a property tabstripPlacement, which can accept one of these values:

  • top - tabs are placed on top side
  • right - tabs are placed on right side
  • bottom - tabs are placed on bottom side
  • left - tabs are placed on left side
